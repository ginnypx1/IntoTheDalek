Consciousness Link:

You find yourself inside a giant dome. Images, moving pictures, are all around you. Most are horrific images of war, carnage, violence.

The Doctor explains that these are the Dalek's memories. He urges you to look away. They will only destroy your soul.

But you see one memory that is shockingly beautiful... You move in closer....

The Doctor explains that you're watching the creation of a star. This could be it. This could be the memory you're looking for...

'I need to form a link to the Dalek brain...', the Doctor explains. 'I need him to think he is seeing this again for the first time.'

'Quick. Tell us a joke. If we both laugh ...I may be able to sync up with his brain.'
