You find yourself inside a metal hallway, black with red lights. There are wires everywhere. You are inside a complicated machine.

The Doctor explains that this room forms the Dalek's cortex vault. Dalek technology designed this vault to suppress any compassion... And also to store all of the Dalek's memories.

There's a simple keypad lock on the Dalek's centeral vault.

'Good news!' The Doctor says. 'Only 3 digits... And the Daleks only know 1s and 0s. This should be fairly easy to crack...'

'But we only get 5 tries, before it shuts down for 24 hours.'
